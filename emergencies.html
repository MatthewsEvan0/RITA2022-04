In case of an emergency...

Call 911

Call Public Safety: 585 - 475 - 3333 (v/tty)

Text Public Safety: 585 - 205 - 8333

When should I call 911 or Public Safety?

If you feel there is a medical emergency, do not hesitate to call 911.

If you suspect someone has consumed too much alcohol or overdosing, don't leave them to "sleep it off". Alcohol poisoning and drug overdoses can kill. The Good Samaritan Protocol protects the caller, the person in need of assistance, and any witnesses involved from receiving disciplinary sanction.

For any non-medical emergencies, please call or text Public Safety.
