Community Standbys

RIT Ambulance provides a stand-by service for events on the RIT campus by request. All requests will be reviewed and where appropriate, be staffed by an EMT fully equipped with emergency equipment. The stand-by may also include an ambulance depending on the staffing needs of the event.

Reserve RIT Ambulance

For questions related to standby requests, please contact ritaevent@rit.edu.

Training Classes

RIT Ambulance is proud to offer AHA and AHSI accredited training classes to members of the RIT Community! (Coming Soon)

Some of these classes will include:

  • Heartsaver® CPR AED
  • Heartsaver® First Aid
  • Heartsaver® First Aid CPR AED
  • Heartsaver® Pediatric First Aid CPR AED
  • Bloodborne Pathogens
  • Basic Life Support (BLS) CPR & AED Training for Healthcare Professionals
  • Bleeding Control Basics (Stop The Bleed)

Registration will be open to RIT Students, Staff and Faculty soon.

For more information, please visit our Community Education page

Seminars

Our members are happy to provide educational seminars to RIT groups on campus. We are able to tailor our presentations to your audience. Whether it is basic first aid, safe drinking, or hands-only CPR, our team can deliver an interactive presentation to your club, team, department, floor, or even greek organization.

For more information about what we can do for your organization or to sign-up for a speaking engagement, please contact our Public Relations Director at ritapr@mail.rit.edu.

Alumni Relations

We do our best to keep our alumni up to date with current project and happenings by way of our Alumni Newsletter. We also love giving tours to our past members to show off just how much we have grown as an agency.

To be added to our alumni newsletter or to schedule a tour, please contact our Public Relations Director at ritapr@mail.rit.edu.
