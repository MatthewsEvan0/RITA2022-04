Thank you for your interest in joining RIT Ambulance!

There is no prior experience necessary to join our agency – we provide everything you’ll need in-house. Additional training can be coordinated through our training department as needed for certifications like EMT-Basic. You may begin training as a Driver or if you are a New York State Certified Emergency Medical Technician, you may begin training as a Medic.

For questions regarding membership and joining, please contact our Recruitment and Retention Director. For questions about our training process, please contact our Training Director.

Before you may submit an application for membership, you must first attend a short information session.
