Standbys

RIT Ambulance provides a stand-by service for events on the RIT campus by request. All requests will be reviewed and where appropriate, be staffed by an EMT fully equipped with emergency equipment. The stand-by may also include an ambulance depending on the staffing needs of the event.

If RIT Ambulance is already dedicated to another event, we will coordinate and contract another ambulance to provide service for your event.

As of 8/1/2017, All standby requests will be handled through the RIT reservation system which can be found at reserve.rit.edu

Reserve RIT Ambulance
